# classic-cups-menu
QR menu

## Files in this folder
- menu.html   → the customer-facing menu (this is what the QR code opens)
- admin.html  → your daily management panel (you use this on your phone/tablet)
- README.txt  → this file

---

## Step 1 – Put the menu online (Netlify – easiest, free)

1. Open https://app.netlify.com/drop in your browser
2. Drag the entire "classic-cups" folder onto the page
3. Wait ~10 seconds — you get a link like: https://amazing-name-123.netlify.app
4. Your menu is now live at: https://amazing-name-123.netlify.app/menu.html

---

## Step 2 – Generate the QR code

1. Go to https://qr.io  (or any free QR generator)
2. Paste your menu link: https://amazing-name-123.netlify.app/menu.html
3. Download the QR code image
4. Print and laminate — place on each table and at the counter

---

## Step 3 – Daily use (Admin panel)

Open admin.html directly in your phone/tablet browser (no internet needed).
- Toggle items ON/OFF for the day
- Tap 📷 next to any item to add a food photo from your phone
- Tap "Save Changes" — the menu page updates immediately

IMPORTANT: The admin panel and menu page must be opened in the SAME browser
on the SAME device. The data is stored in that browser's memory.

If you want the menu to update across devices, you'll need to host the
admin panel on Netlify too and use the same browser. Or just keep one
dedicated tablet/phone at the counter for admin.

---

## Step 4 – Bookmark the admin panel

On your phone:
- Open admin.html in Chrome/Safari
- Tap Share → "Add to Home Screen"
- It appears like an app on your phone — open it every morning to update the menu
